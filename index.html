📥 Import broker CSV
How to get your CSV from your broker
  1. Log in to your broker platform
  2. Find the Export or Download option in your account history
  3. Download all your trade history as a CSV
  4. Among the files provided, select the one named Closed Trades or Closed Positions
⚠️ Important: Make sure to select the Closed Trades file — not the open positions, not the notifications log. Only closed trades contain the final P&L needed for the journal.
After import, each trade opens individually in the form so you can complete the session, pattern, emotion and notes before saving.
